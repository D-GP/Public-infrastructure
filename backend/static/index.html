How the Platform Works

A streamlined process designed for transparency and rapid resolution.

1. Spot & Capture

Notice a broken public asset? Open the app, take a clear photo, and provide a brief description of the issue.

2. Pin Location

The system automatically tags the exact GPS coordinates to help maintenance teams locate the issue quickly.

3. Direct Routing

Your report is intelligently routed to the relevant local body and department authority based on the category and location.

4. Track Resolution

Receive SMS and email updates as authorities review, action, and successfully resolve your reported complaint.
